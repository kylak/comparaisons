Βαραχίας, ου, ὁ : Nom, Masculin
EN 1 : Barachiah, the father of a man killed in the temple
EN 2 : Barachiah; his identity is uncertain, perhaps father of the Zechariah killed by the Zealots in the last Jewish War.

Βαραχίας d'origine hébraïque (בֶּרֶכְיָה), Barachias (c-à-d Berechijah), un Israélite : Barachias.
