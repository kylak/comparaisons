Σεβαστός, ή, όν : Adjectif
EN 1 : reverend, august, Augustus, a Roman emperor
EN 2 : (official Greek equivalent of Augustus), venerated, august, a title of the Roman emperors; hence secondarily: Augustan, imperial.
